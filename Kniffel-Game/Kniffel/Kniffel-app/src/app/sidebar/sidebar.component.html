Goal

The aim of Kniffel is to score the most points by throwing certain combinations of cubes.

Roll the dice

At the start of your turn, you can press the “Roll” button to throw the dice. You can roll the dice a total of 3 times per round

Holding cubes

After your first roll, click on a cube to “hold” it. The held cube does not change in your next roll.

Click on the cube again to bring it back in game.

Select categories

After you have rolled the dice, you can click on the field to the right of the category to collect the points

Points

To receive points, the condition for the corresponding category must be fulfilled. You receive a different number of points for each category.
